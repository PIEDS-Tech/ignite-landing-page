About IGNITE 2025
6 Days. 100 Innovators. 20+ MVPs.
Whether you’re a student or an early-stage founder, IGNITE takes you from idea → MVP through a high-intensity blend of workshops, mentorship, and hands-on building — right from the beaches of Goa.
Ideate
Workshops and expert sessions to validate your idea, sharpen your problem statement, and align your team around a clear vision.
Build
Hands-on building sprints with 1-on-1 mentorship from founders and operators to take your concept from validated idea to working MVP.
Launch
Present your MVP on Demo Day in front of investors, mentors, and the wider startup community — and compete for equity-free grants.
